About me,

Ever since I was a child beauty stood out to me in a way that made me feel alive. This has led me from the valleys of Yosemite to the edges of the cosmos in an effort to keep a small part of it.

My interest lies in sharing this view of mine through my photos, in the past it has been astro, macro, digital, and most recently film photography. I like to explore technologies and test their limits while keeping my vision clear

I find these challenges of learning new technology exciting and rewarding, and dedicate all of myself into adapting as they come.

I am currently persuing a degree in computer science to help pesent me with the opertunities to create and share my works

The rest can be found in the intricacies of my work
